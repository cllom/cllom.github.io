Fig.1 The miniature car made out of silver

Electric VechileWelding

September 2020

It began when I went to China and I have always dreamed of making my own electric scooter. So I decided to collect all the parts like the brushless motor, tires and the controller.

Fig.2 My Flight Laguage from Shanghai, China to Jakarta, Indonesia.

Electric scooters are very common in Shanghai, you could always see people roaming with electric vehicles. Before coming back to Indonesia because of the Coronavirus, I decided to finish my project in Jakarta, so I brought and filled my language with all the materials that I had bought.

Fig.3 Fail Attempt

Firstly, I started to weld it but it seems that the spring I founded was too weak and then later I founded an unused motor electric bike chassis. I decided just to modify and weld the chassis together with the wheel holder and steering wheel.

Fig.3 Wiring Up

After the mechanical parts were done, I started to wiring up all the power electronics. It was not easy to find the documentation about the controller, most of them are in Chinese. Lucky my dad could translate most of them. I started to wiring it up.

Fig.5 First Test

This is my actual first test, it went pretty smooth. Most satisfying, I used this the most almost everyday to travel in short distances because it could only run with the maximum speed of 30 km/h.
