1. Introduction

The Stewart platform (SP) has been a popular research topic in robotics since its first appearance on the scientific agenda in 1965 in the renown work by Stewart [1].

Four Degree of Freedom Stewart Platfrom by Marcello Tania.

I am inspired after surfing several steawart platforms on the internet.They have shown a very interesting application and I have founded that most of the people has created their own platform and most of them are not open source. Therefore I think it would be great if I could design this platform and share it to universities students who would like to learn robotics. It will save their time regarding and we can caliborate and share our knowledge in the future.

Inspiration from universities student projects and hobbiests.

There are too much variations and we need to caliborate by using a open source platform to give the best result!

To make the best result, I think we can all caliborate and share our knowlege between university students or hobbiest. My final goal is to share and learn from other people who are also interested building this platform.

Skills used in the project:

  • 3D CAD
  • 3D printing
  • 2D Design and production
  • Electronics design
  • Electronics production
  • Embeded Programming

Advantages of this platform:

  • Low Cost Design
  • Robust and rigidity
  • High speed
  • Large payload
  • Precision
  • Embeded Programming

3. Electronics Production

There are 7 PCBs that I was planning to make 6 for sticking the stepper drivers and 1 for the the micro controller and the main power supply.

Unfortunetly, I did a silly mistake by not double checking the connection for the driver and has wasted all the material and time. So I could not finish my pcb on time.But I have decided to use my old pcb board and the bread board to connect everything.

Furthermore, I have failed making the pcb and I guest the reason was I did not design my pcb nicely such as the wires connection are too near to each other and the most important also we have to stick the copper pcb nicely to CNC milling machine.
